About Us

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

The BookSwap Story

We believe in the transformative power of books. Books can open our minds to new ideas, teach us about different cultures, and help us to connect with others. Our platform welcomes all book enthusiasts.


We want to make books more accessible to everyone. Not everyone can afford to buy new books, and many people simply don't have the space to store them. BookSwap allows people to share their books with others, making them more affordable and sustainable.

Frequently asked questions.

Have a different question and can’t find the answer you’re looking for?

What is BookSwap?

BookSwap is an online platform that allows individuals living in close proximity to exchange books with each other.

How does BookSwap work?

BookSwap works by facilitating book exchanges between two individuals who live near each other.

How do I list my books on BookSwap?

Listing your books on BookSwap is simple. After creating an account, navigate to your profile and click on the "Add Book" button.

How do I initiate a book exchange?

To initiate a book exchange, find a book you'd like to swap and click on it to view the details. On the book's page, you'll find an option to message the owner.

Is there a cost to use BookSwap?

No, BookSwap is a free platform. There are no charges for creating an account, listing your books, or engaging in book exchanges.
